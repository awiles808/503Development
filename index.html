Who is 503?

503 Development Company was founded by Owner/Acquisitions Manager Michael Giraud in order to provide homeowners a fair and reliable way to sell their property fast even in a challenging real estate market. 503 Development Company is a licensed residential contractor in the State of Oregon that specializes in buying houses in “as is” condition. We are constantly looking to buy homes that are in need of minor repair and updating all the way to complete tear downs. In addition we also purchase smaller homes within high end neighborhoods throughout the Portland Metro area. 503 Development Company is a licensed and bonded residential contractor in the State of Oregon that specializes in renovating classic older PDX homes, as well as building new in-fill homes throughout Portland’s most desirable neighborhoods. We are a growing Design/Build Firm looking to provide homeowners a fair and reliable way to sell their home fast and in “as is” condition.
